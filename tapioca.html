Mango Cream Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup small tapioca pearls
  • 2 ripe mangoes (diced)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Cook Tapioca: Boil water, add tapioca pearls, and cook until translucent (10-15 min). Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. Prepare Base: In a bowl, mix coconut milk, condensed milk, sugar, and vanilla. Stir well.
  3. Combine: Add cooked tapioca and diced mangoes to the mixture. Stir and chill for at least 30 min.
