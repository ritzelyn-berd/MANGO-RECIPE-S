Mango Cream Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until well combined.
  2. Press the mixture into a lined 8x8-inch pan.
  3. Drizzle condensed milk evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle chocolate chips and nuts on top (if using).
  5. Chill in the fridge for 1-2 hours until firm.
  6. Cut into bars.
